# Blockd Privacy Policy

## Short version

Blockd itself does not use accounts, analytics, tracking, ads, servers, or external services. Your blocking setup and Blockd progress stats stay on your iPhone.

Apple, iOS, App Store Connect, and TestFlight may process their own operational data when you install, test, or submit feedback through Apple services. This policy describes Blockd's behavior, not Apple's.

Effective date: May 8, 2026

Blockd is a local iPhone Screen Time utility that helps you block selected apps and websites with quick blocks, focus timers, schedules, Deep Work, and temporary unlock friction.

### Data Blockd Stores Locally

Blockd stores app state locally on your device so the app and its Screen Time extensions can work. This can include:

- selected blocking targets from Apple's Screen Time picker
- manually entered website domains
- active and scheduled blocking sessions
- temporary unlock state and the reason you typed for that unlock
- local Blockd progress stats, such as protected time, completed focus timers, blocking streaks, schedule usage, and blocked shield encounters

This information is stored locally using Apple's app storage and App Group storage. It is used to run Blockd's blocking, shielding, scheduling, unlock, and local stats features.

### Data Blockd Does Not Collect

Blockd itself does not:

- create accounts
- collect analytics
- use advertising or tracking SDKs
- sell personal data
- send your Blockd state or stats to a server
- use external services for app functionality
- use push notifications or remote Live Activity updates

### Apple Screen Time Permission

Blockd uses Apple's Screen Time APIs, including Family Controls, Device Activity, and Managed Settings, to let you choose apps and websites to shield. Apple shows its own permission prompt. The Screen Time picker is designed so Blockd can apply your selected targets without exposing more detail than needed for the app's local blocking behavior.

### TestFlight and Apple Services

If you install Blockd through TestFlight, Apple may process crash reports, installation information, feedback, diagnostics, and other operational data under Apple's terms and privacy policies. Feedback you choose to send through TestFlight is handled by Apple and shared with the developer through App Store Connect.

### Support

If you contact support at ejosterman@gmail.com, we will receive the information you choose to send, such as your email address, message, screenshots, or diagnostic details. Support messages are used only to respond to your request and improve Blockd.

### Children

Blockd is not currently marketed as a children's app. Screen Time features may be used by individuals on their own devices. Do not use Blockd for another person's device unless you have the legal right and appropriate consent to do so.

### Changes

We may update this policy as Blockd changes. If Blockd adds accounts, analytics, tracking, cloud sync, purchases, or external services in the future, this policy and App Store privacy disclosures should be updated before those changes are released.
